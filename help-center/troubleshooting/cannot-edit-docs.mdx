---
title: Cannot Edit Documentation
description: Resolve issues when you can't edit pages.
---

## Symptoms
- "Edit" button is disabled or missing
- Changes are not saved

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Ensure you are logged in and have editor access for the project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh Session">
    Log out, then log back in. Sometimes access tokens expire.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser Cache">
    Clear your browser's cache and reload the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
