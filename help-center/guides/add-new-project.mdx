---
title: Adding a New Project
description: How to start a new documentation project.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Log in to your Tomislav Soskic account and open the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click the "Add Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill out your project name and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Press "Create" to finish setup. Your new space is ready!
  </Step>
</Steps>
